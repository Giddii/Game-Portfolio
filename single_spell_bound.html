Single Spell Bound

Single Spell Bound Web Build

By Studio Magnets

Developed using C# and Unity.

Controls

  • WASD to Move
  • Left click with mouse to Shoot

    • Single Spell Bound is a single-player top-down action-adventure game centered around a wizard who can only use his wand to cast one single spell. Knock your enemies and yourself with this spell to explore and escape the perilous icy dungeon along with your companion, Morpheus.

    My Contributions: I helped contribute to the game design mechanics and level design. While I helped with other things in the game, I mainly programmed the doors, enemy element typing, puzzle interactables, and transitions. I also worked on the programming SFX side of the game.
